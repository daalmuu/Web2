low-Calories Tiramisu

Recipe Creator
Maria Rossi
Dessert

A light and healthy low-calorie tiramisu, made with creamy Greek yogurt, finely ground oats, strong black coffee, and a touch of natural honey. Perfect for anyone looking for a guilt-free dessert, and ideal for a light treat or after-meal sweet.
